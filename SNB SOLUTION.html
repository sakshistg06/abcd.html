THE BASICS: HOW SOLAR WORKS

Collection: During the day, sunlight is converted to DC electricity by the Panels on the rooftop.
Conversion: This electricity travels to the inverter where it is stabilized and hence fit to serve your home's electricity needs.
Usage: The system now becomes a source of Cleaner and Economical electricity production. This system set up essentially remains the same for nearly for all sizes and capacities, what changes is the number of components and the capacity of each component.

THE BEAUTY OF NET METERING


Thanks to the concept of net metering, your present electricity line acts as a storage for the electricity your panels produce! This means, that when we design our system, it produces enough electricity for your day and night consumption during the day itself, the extra units produced during the day flows back into the grid and is then used at night This Means that though your solar system depends on the sun, you don't have to for having a 24x7 interruption free electricity supply!
